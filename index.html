The company

Manred International Inc.

Manred International Inc. is a Panama-based software development company building intelligent applications for sports communities worldwide. Our mission is to create tools that make competitive sports management accessible, precise, and engaging for administrators and players alike.

PadelProMania is our flagship product — built from the ground up by players, for players, with a focus on mathematical precision, great user experience, and the kind of competitive engagement that keeps leagues thriving.
